About EduFund

EduFund is revolutionizing how students discover and apply for scholarships. Founded with a mission to make education accessible to all, we leverage cutting-edge AI technology to match students with relevant opportunities and streamline the application process.

Our platform eliminates the tedious work of searching through countless scholarships and completing repetitive applications. We analyze thousands of scholarships daily, matching them to your unique profile and automatically submitting applications on your behalf.

Since our founding, we've helped students secure over $15 million in scholarship funding, making higher education a reality for thousands who might otherwise face financial barriers.
